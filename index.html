Learn about Omani ships

Sohar Ship

The Sohar ship is a traditional Omani vessel renowned for its historical voyage tracing the ancient maritime trade routes to China, symbolizing Oman's seafaring legacy and adventurous spirit.

Zeenat Al-Bihar

Zeenat Al-Bihar is a traditional Omani ship known for its elegance and historical significance, symbolizing Oman's rich maritime heritage and craftsmanship.

Jewel of Muscat

The Jewel of Muscat is a traditional Omani ship, meticulously reconstructed to reflect ancient maritime craftsmanship and Oman's rich seafaring history.

Al Shu’ai ship

The Al Shu’ai ship is a traditional Omani vessel primarily used for pearl diving and fishing, reflecting Oman's rich maritime heritage and its deep connection to the sea.

Forts Al-Jalali and Al-Mirani and Omani Ships

The forts of Al-Jalali and Al-Mirani stand as historical guardians overlooking the sea, telling tales of Omani naval prowess and rich heritage. The ships that once graced these waters reflect a storied past and Oman’s maritime legacy.
